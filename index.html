Blix.co

Blix.co was born out of the frustration that most brands look the same, sound the same, and disappear just as fast. Founded by a creator-turned-strategist who spent years building identities behind the scenes, Blix was created to serve those who refuse to be generic. What started as a one-person studio crafting personal brands for founders and influencers has grown into a collective of designers, storytellers, and strategists obsessed with timeless identity. At Blix, we don’t just deliver work — we invest in your vision. We take the time to understand your truth, your taste, and your trajectory. Our process is intentionally deep, personal, and honest, because we believe your brand should not only be seen — it should be remembered. Every client we work with becomes part of our culture. You’re not a transaction. You’re a legacy in progress.
